## Project Description (short)

A physical-to-digital NFT card battle game where players photograph their real Gunpla model kits and mint them as playable ERC-721 battle cards with lore-accurate stats on Base. Pokemon-style turn-based battles with $GNDM token staking.

## Project Description (detailed)

GundariuM turns real-world Gunpla model kits into on-chain battle cards. Players photograph their kits, select the matching Mobile Suit from a curated database of 148 suits spanning the entire Gundam universe, choose their kit grade (which determines rarity and stat multipliers), and mint an ERC-721 NFT card with on-chain traits — name, series, faction, pilot, weapons, armor type, HP, and damage values.

Cards are used in Pokemon-style turn-based PVP and PVE battles with $GNDM token staking. The battle system features strategic weapon selection (4 weapons available every turn), armor-type matchups (I-Field blocks beam, Phase Shift blocks melee, etc.), and grade-based power scaling from Common (SD, 0.7x) to Legendary (PG, 1.5x).

The Cosmetics AI lets players digitally paint their cards and add custom decals — a feature that naturally works best with real Gunpla photos, creating an organic quality incentive without gatekeeping.

## How does this bring new users to Base?

GundariuM targets a massive audience that is largely not on-chain yet: Gunpla builders. Bandai sells 600M+ kits worldwide. The subreddit r/Gunpla has 300K+ members. Instagram #gunpla has 5M+ posts. These are hobbyists who already own the "NFT" on their shelf — we're asking them to bring their collection on-chain.

The onboarding funnel is uniquely low-friction: photograph something you're already proud of → mint it as a battle card → play. There's no "buy a random JPEG" barrier. Every card represents a real kit the player built with their hands.

We're also a registered Farcaster miniapp, making GundariuM discoverable in-feed to the existing Base/Farcaster community. The Base App MiniApp integration is planned for launch.

Full launch across all features — Mint, Arena, Leaderboard, Prize Pools — is May 10, 2026.
